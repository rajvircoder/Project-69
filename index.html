Germany

I Had visited in April 17 2020

Germany,officially the Federal Republic of Germany,is a country in Central Europe. It is the second-most populous country in Europe after Russia, and the most populous member state of the European Union. Germany is situated between the Baltic and North seas to the north, and the Alps to the south. Its 16 constituent states are bordered by Denmark to the north, Poland and the Czech Republic to the east, Austria and Switzerland to the south, and France, Luxembourg, Belgium, and the Netherlands to the west. The nation's capital and most populous city is Berlin and its main financial centre is Frankfurt; the largest urban area is the Ruhr.

Review On The Movies

Sponge Bob
It is a fun filled movie

I watched this movie and it is very funny and there is a sponge man who lives under the sea in a pineapple house.Here's a rundown: the show has 10 main characters, including the titular sponge. The others are: Patrick the starfish, who lives under a rock; a surly octopus named Squidward; Mr. Krabs, a stingy crab restaurateur; a whale named Pearl who is somehow the crab's daughter; a Texan squirrel in scuba gear, named Sandy; Mrs. Puff, a stressed-out pufferfish who is SpongeBob's teacher; an evil plankton named Plankton; Plankton's snarky computer, named Karen; and SpongeBob's pet, Gary the sea snail, who meows like a house cat.


Lion King
It is a story about Simba, a cub.

"The Lion King" has been one of my favorite Disney animated films, so I was nervous about seeing Jon Favreau's remake. Surprisingly, from beginning to end, I found myself consistently enjoying this remake more than the original, and I had no problem with it. This remake absorbed my attention with it's astounding, dazzling visuals, and this is the only Disney lifelike remake that has no humans, and animals are my passion. Jon Favreau and his crew pulled it off so well, and how on earth did they do it?
